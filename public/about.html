About Pak Debate Forum

Building confident thinkers, ethical speakers, and future leaders through the power of debate.

Who We Are

Pak Debate Forum (PDF) is a youth-led intellectual platform dedicated to promoting critical thinking, public speaking, and respectful discourse across Pakistan.

We believe debate is more than competition — it is a discipline that sharpens minds, builds confidence, and prepares individuals to lead with clarity and purpose.

What We Do

🗣 Debate Events

We organize high-quality parliamentary and policy debate events, providing students with real competitive exposure.

📚 Learning Resources

From guides to workshops, we help debaters grow beyond the podium.

🤝 Community Building

We connect passionate minds and cultivate a respectful debate culture nationwide.

Why It Matters

In a world of noise, debate teaches structure. In disagreement, it teaches respect. In youth, it builds leadership.

Pak Debate Forum exists to ensure that Pakistani youth are not only heard — but understood.
